I am Interested

What is Lean Vietnam Boot Camp?

"Build Fast, Learn Fast, Fail Cheap" is a 2-day Boot Camp to help you discover how lean impacts profit, quality, customer service and fosters innovation across your organization. After this boot camp you will understand and know how to develop a strategy for successfully applying lean enterprise principles. Learn the most common lean tools and techniques and how to apply them using rapid process improvement (Kaizen) events.

Why Lean Vietnam Boot Camp?

Lean Vietnam is tailored and designed based on know-how, best of breed and local expertise to help Vietnamese companies to achieve growth in their business, improve productivity to waste less and being innovative to have sustainable growth.Every leader in any organization has the same impediments and is thriving to improve these hurdles.However not every leader or company succeed. Not every company manage to overcome these barriers and achieve the business nirvana called “innovation”. Why is these failures happening? We have worked and observed with various firms in different countries, varying cultures and different industries, some of these companies are HSBC, SEB, ON Semi, Nuance, Navigos Group, VietnamWorks and Infospeech. We have found a repeatable pattern among these great firms. We believe we found the problem.

The problem is, most companies today are run to minimize rick, not maximize freedom and speed.

  • Information and data is hoarded, not shared.

  • Their design is a vestige of an era when failure was expensive and deliberation was a virtue

  • Decision making power lies in the hands of the few

Key Benefits

  1. Make rapid and dramatic performance improvements to any process.
  2. Build more effective and unified cross-functional teams through joint improvement efforts.
  3. Generate savings today that can fund your long-term transformation efforts.
  4. Build leaders for change as kaizen facilitators.
  5. Develop a culture for aggressive improvement.

Who should attend?

CTO(s), Vice presidents, directors, managers of IT organizations in charge of managing company resources and delivering business results.
